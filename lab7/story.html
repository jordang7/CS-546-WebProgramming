The time I spilled water all over my laptop

A Very Stressful Night

I'm doing homework a class where I needed a linux console and I was making good progress. I was almost done but still had some work to go. It was a long assignment.I got a glass of water and went to put it down, but I put it down on an unlevel surface, and proceeded to spill an entire cup of water on my laptop. So not only did I just break my thousand dollar laptop, but I also lost all my progress on the homework and had no way to continue working on it haha. I ended up getting borrowing one of my friends laptops and finishing it but it was stressful.
